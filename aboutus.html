ABOUT US

For over two decades, FBA has been a family owned and operated business started by Anthony who has been a Nashville resident for over 25 years. He started out working for family and friends and as word of mouth quickly spread, they acquired a full workload within a very short period of time. FBA was created and has been running strong ever since, providing top quality fencing for the greater Nashville area. With our customers’ satisfaction as our primary concern, over half of all of our clients are either repeat or referred customers. From our unmatched 5 year warranty, to performing turn-key projects, FBA has become one of the premiere fence companies in the state of Tennessee. Our favorite project is always our next, so let FBA provide an unmatched customer experience for you.

WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT?

 	 Unique On or Off-Site Estimates 

 	 FBA offers free on or off site estimates. We utilize satellite measuring technology which gives us 
 	 the ability to create your estimate remotely using detailed aerial photography to generate accurate measurements

 	 visualization of how your choice fence project may appear. Use of our off-site estimations is also helpful if

Decks - Composite Material

Custom wood or composite decks add valuable outdoor living space to your home. Our decks are constructed of either treated wood or composite material. All deck framing is wood and, at least, with 2×10′s and 6×6 posts. Each material has advantages and disadvantages. Here are some things to consider:

An easy way to consider your deck dimensions is to consider different lumber sizes and measure the area of the desired deck. Lumber is sold in 8′-10′-12′-14′-16′ increments. Longer sizes are available, but can get expensive. Try to match your idea with this in mind so that the least amount of wood is wasted to minimize costs.

Wooden Fences

Wood is the most versatile material for building a fence. With many styles to choose from, wood fencing provides the privacy you need and the protection for your pets and family.

Benefits of a wood fence:

  • • It easily accepts stains and paints, adding warmth and beauty to your property.

  • • It is available in a variety of sizes and grades, wood fence systems permit unique designs to match and compliment your property.

  • • It naturally weathers, giving wood an economical advantage.

  • • It provides security, defines boundaries, protects property, and helps control noise, wind and sunlight.

Below some popular examples of wood fencing, however virtually any combination of each could be built. Also, FBA would be happy to entertain any other particular style that exists or comes to mind.

Wood Privacy Fence

Our privacy fencing is the most economical way to create a private setting in your back yard. Generally, 4×4 posts are used with 2×4 framing and 5/8x6x6 dog eared pickets are used. Gothic, French Gothic and rounded dog eared pickets are also available. We never use pre-made sections of wood fencing due to their poor quality and short longevity. Semi-privacy can be easily made by spacing the pickets

Dog-Ear Privacy Fence

Our Dog ear fencing is the most economical way to create a private setting in your back yard. Generally, 4×4 posts are used with (3)2×4 framing and 5/8x6x6 dog eared pickets are used. Semi-privacy can easily be made by spacing the pickets. If you don’t see the particular style that you are looking for on our website, the possibilities are endless. Please contact us with any questions. We are open to new ideas and/or projects that may not be found on our website

Table Top & Stair Table Top Privacy Fence

Each section of this fence is square and level to the earth. A stair step conforms to the contour of the land. This type of fence is not recommended for excessively slopped yards because height will be lost. The trim on top covers dog ear pickets for a more finished look.

Extras: Post caps, 6×6 posts

Rolling Table Top Fence

Each section of the rolling table top fence is custom built to “roll” with the contour of the land. This type of fence is recommended when maximum height is desired. The trim on the top covers dog ear pickets for a more finished look.

Extras: Post caps, 6×6 posts

Scalloped Privacy Fence

A scalloped wood privacy fence is constructed similarly to a dog ear fence. However, the top is cut with a dip or “scallop” between the posts. Framing can be built between the posts for a more finished look.

Extras: Gothic or French gothic posts

Arched Privacy Fence

An arched fence is constructed similarly to a dog ear fence. However, the top of the fence is cut with an arch between the posts. Framing can be built between the posts for a more finished look.

Extras: Gothic or French gothic posts

Gothic or French Gothic Privacy Fence

Gothic or French Gothic pickets can be used instead of dog ear for a more artistic or classic look.

Shadow Box Privacy Fence

Shadow Box fences provide 100% privacy when looking directly toward them, but spacing allows light to come through at an angle. We recommend this style when the same look from either side is desired.

Horizontal Privacy Fence

Horizontal fences have the boards running side to side as opposed to straight up and down. They can either be level with the earth or sloped with the land.
